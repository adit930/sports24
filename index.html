ABOUT PRABALYA

Objective.

The sports committee is to promote every individuals health, physical well-being as well as the acquistion of physical skills among the students. The committee aspires to inculcate qualities such as sportsmanship, team spirit and bonding. It is designed to serve the interests of the student community competitive sports and other recreational activities i.e. both indoor and outdoor.

THEME

Greek Mythology

Step into the realm of gods and heroes as Prabalya, our sports fest, unveils the majestic world of Greek mythology. In an epic scavenger hunt, 8 schools at NMIMS will battle for supremacy, embodying the spirit of gods and goddesses. The top three schools will ascend the podium as Zeus, Poseidon, and Hades, adding a mythic twist to the triumphs of athletic prowess. Let the games begin, where legends are born!

Zeus

Zeus, the king of the Greek gods, is a figure of immense power and authority. He is the god of the sky and thunder, and he is often depicted as a bearded man wielding a lightning bolt. He is the father of many gods and heroes, and he is also known for his many affairs with both goddesses and mortal women.

Poseidon

Poseidon, the god of the sea, earthquakes, storms, and horses, is one of the most powerful and feared gods in Greek mythology. He is also known for his wisdom and justice, and as the protector of seafarers and the guardian of many Hellenic cities and colonies.

Hades

Hades, the Greek god of the underworld, is a figure of immense power and mystery. He is the ruler of the realm of the dead, but he is also a protector of the dead and a giver of wealth. Hades is a reminder of the inevitability of death, but he is also a symbol of hope and rebirth.
